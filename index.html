About Me

Hello! I'm Polina, a passionate graphic designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creating impactful visual experiences. My mission is to craft engaging and innovative visual content that tells compelling stories and connects with audiences on a meaningful level.

Beyond design, I specialize in illustration and digital content creation, maintaining a lifestyle blog where I share my creative insights and journey. Jazz Festival Poster

This poster is entirely fictitious and was created in Photoshop. The idea came to me by accident. I was reading an article about Shepard Fairey's work and was inspired by his most famous piece: Barack Obama's 'Hope' poster.

I wanted to create my own version, and using this technique for a jazz festival poster seemed like the perfect solution. The color scheme imparts a timeless quality to the poster while also evoking a sense of nostalgia, much like jazz music itself.

Photoshop Poster Design Typography

Gaming Mouse Advertisement

This is a potential advertising banner for a gaming mouse. The banner itself was created in Photoshop, and the logo was designed in Illustrator.

The design is inspired by the features of the product. The bottom of the mouse features a vibrant, multi-colored backlight, emphasizing its modern technology and versatile capabilities. In the poster, I aimed to convey the sensation that using this mouse transports you to another dimension.

Photoshop Illustrator Product Design

Magazine Layout Design

This is a mock-up of a magazine page featuring a Finnish article discussing the rising popularity of healthy juices in New York.

The task was to visually represent the article's content through photographs and include a small amount of text on three pages. I aimed to create a vibrant and refreshing layout, reminiscent of a fresh smoothie. Additionally, I highlighted key points in the text by altering the font.
